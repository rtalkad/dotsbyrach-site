About the artist

Rachna

Dot mandala art is patience made visible. What looks like intricate lace is really thousands of single dots, placed one after another with a fine tool, layer by layer, until a pattern emerges that couldn't have been planned all at once.

Rachna works from Bengaluru, drawing on festival colour, temple geometry, and everyday moments of calm. Each piece — whether a small keychain or a large wall panel — is entirely hand-dotted, no stencils, no shortcuts.
